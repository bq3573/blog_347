Website Builders

Square space is one of many website creators that allows users to make their own websites without having to code on their own. This is a very interesting subject to me since I am in the process of learning web development myself. I decided to read up on some reviews and how it works. Squarespace works by providing templates and styles to their users. It is a “drag-and-drop website builder” that allows you to build a website in sections at a time. The website appears exactly how it looks in the website editor interface. The websites you can make are very clean and professional and once you get the hang of the editor anyone can create their own website. The article I read says that Squarespace isn’t super user friendly but still is positively reviewed with a little time spent learning how to use it and stands up as a frontrunner in the space of website builders. Website builders are very important in a world where everything is digital. Allowing people without coding knowledge to create websites on their own is a tremendous service to provide. I believe that the biggest advantages are seen by small businesses and individuals wanting to promote themselves.

Squarespace also provides a number of tutorials for beginner users. The tutorial I read about gave some information on HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Git. You can actually make changes to the underlying templates that exist on Squarespace customizing your website even more. This is done through “Developer Mode.” I think this is where someone who does have experience in web development can leverage Squarespace to be an even more powerful tool.

All in all I see tools like Squarespace as super signifigant to the world of web development. Providing access to many people who otherwise would not be able to create a space for themseleves online, more access is a good thing and I think everyone benefits from that.
